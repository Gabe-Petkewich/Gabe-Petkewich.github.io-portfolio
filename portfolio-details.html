fridge




Description

A flutter project that was designed with HCI principles in mind to provide a platform allowing users to keep track of items in their fridge using the Spoonacular API. By tracking expiration dates, suggesting currently feasible recipes, and storing favorites for next time, fridge was an app built to improve the quality of life in the kitchen. The team was composed of Nicholas Hadley, Woo Jung, Atakan Cocen, and myself.


Technology

Flutter, Dart, Firebase, Ruby
