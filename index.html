About Me

My name is Gabriel Petkewich and I am a Full Stack Software Developer from Statesboro, Georgia. In December 2020 I will graduate from Georgia Southern University with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. At Southern I've been an active member of the Aurora Game Development Club and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).

During my time at Georgia Southern, I've worked for almost 3 years at Morris Technology as a Junior Solutions Developer. Here I've added many skills and tools to my personal web development kit: Django, AWS, PSQL, and more!

Outside of software development I'm a drummer and live music enthusiast. I love cats, my favorite video game is Rocket League, and some of my favorite bands include Polyphia, Hippo Campus, and Weezer.
